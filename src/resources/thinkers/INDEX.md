## By Framing Pattern Detected

Use this section when you identify *how* an article frames its subject, not just *what* it covers.

### "The market decided" / Market outcomes presented as natural
Load: [karl-marx.md](karl-marx.md) (commodity fetishism), [lafargue.md](paul-lafargue.md) (property as historical, not natural), [lukacs.md](georg-lukacs.md) (reification — social relations appearing as thing-like facts)

### "Great leader" / Events attributed to individual decisions
Load: [georgi-plekhanov.md](georgi-plekhanov.md) (critique of great-man history), [marx.md](karl-marx.md) (structural forces behind political events), [trotsky.md](leon-trotsky.md) (class forces shaping events, with individuals as expressions of those forces)

### "National unity" / Bipartisan consensus / Cross-class appeals
Load: [connolly.md](james-connolly.md) (national interest = owning-class interest), [roy.md](mn-roy.md) (cross-class alliances subordinating workers), [gramsci.md](antonio-gramsci.md) (constructed divisions preventing class unity)

### "Reasonable reform" / Incremental change as sufficient
Load: [luxemburg.md](rosa-luxemburg.md) (reform preserving existing order), [deleon.md](daniel-deleon.md) (reform channeling discontent into safe institutions), [draper.md](hal-draper.md) (lesser-evil logic as trap)

### "Extremism on both sides" / Horseshoe framing
Load: [draper.md](hal-draper.md) (socialism from above vs. below — the real distinction), [mattick.md](paul-mattick.md) (democratic self-management erased from the spectrum), [korsch.md](karl-korsch.md) (how critical frameworks are co-opted)

### "Troubled region" / Developing world as inherently chaotic
Load: [padmore.md](george-padmore.md) (colonial extraction behind instability), [mariategui.md](jose-carlos-mariategui.md) (European categories imposed on non-European realities), [james.md](clr-james.md) (erasure of non-white agency)

### "Job creators" / Wealth as merit, poverty as failure
Load: [marx.md](karl-marx.md) (surplus value — wealth created by labor, captured by capital), [lafargue.md](paul-lafargue.md) (work ethic as ideology), [engels.md](friedrich-engels.md) (working-class conditions rendered invisible)

### "Humanitarian intervention" / Military action as benevolence
Load: [lenin.md](vladimir-lenin.md) (imperialism as structural stage, not moral choice), [padmore.md](george-padmore.md) (civilizational rhetoric masking extraction), [luxemburg.md](rosa-luxemburg.md) (militarism as economic necessity)

### "Workers vs. workers" / Gender, race, or immigration as labor-market competition
Load: [zetkin.md](clara-zetkin.md) (capital using gender to divide workers), [padmore.md](george-padmore.md) (racial divisions preventing solidarity), [gramsci.md](antonio-gramsci.md) (ideological construction of divisions)

### "There is no alternative" / Capitalist realism
Load: [korsch.md](karl-korsch.md) (critical theory co-opted into legitimation), [mattick.md](paul-mattick.md) (Keynesian consensus as ideology), [mariategui.md](jose-carlos-mariategui.md) (insisting on alternatives from outside the European frame)

### "Irresponsible radicals" / Labor militancy as disruption
Load: [luxemburg.md](rosa-luxemburg.md) (mass strikes as organic response to exploitation), [deleon.md](daniel-deleon.md) (co-optation of "responsible" leaders), [draper.md](hal-draper.md) (socialism from below as democratic tradition)

### Institutions presented as neutral arbiters
Load: [lenin.md](vladimir-lenin.md) (state as instrument of class domination), [draper.md](hal-draper.md) (state bureaucracy serving class interests), [trotsky.md](leon-trotsky.md) (institutions diverging from stated mission to serve controllers), [james.md](clr-james.md) (institutional interests shaping output regardless of stated mission)
